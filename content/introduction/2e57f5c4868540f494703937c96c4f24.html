Hello!

This course is designed for all personnel who may be required to complete an Incident form in the Accidents and Safety Reporting module. The module serves to, "Record accident and safety incidents, and collect information needed to perform investigations and generate OSHA reports."

The training session is broken into four snippets for which you will need to answer a corresponding question correctly.

You will learn how to draft a new Incident form, complete a an Injury/Illness Activity as well as a Risk Assessment, and the necessary sections to be completed in the subsequent phases of the Accidents & Safety Reporting module. 

Make sure to go through each snippet and pass all assessments in order to get the certificate. 

Let's get started!
